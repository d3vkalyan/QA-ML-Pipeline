You can share your report by generating a private link and sending it to your addressee. Select the Sharing Settings option in the Report Settings menu.

The Sharing Settings menu will appear. Click the Share by link button on the right side of the pop-up window.

You will see the link, which you can copy and send to your clients.

The Allow to reveal provider checkbox defines how detailed will be your report for a quest. Tick it to give more information about the data structure or set it blank for giving minimal info.
